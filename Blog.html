Why continue your education with an online course?
Online courses have made distance learning more accessible than ever – all you need is a computer linked to the internet, and you can study at the University of Cambridge from anywhere in the world. At ICE, we’ve established a strong reputation for quality online learning. We keep class sizes small to maximise interaction between students and tutors, and you’ll receive personal feedback on your work from our leading Cambridge academics. We’re firm believers in the power of peer learning, too, so you’ll have the opportunity to engage in stimulating discussion as part of our global community of learners. What’s more, our part-time courses are designed to flex around the demands of your life. We’re also proud to make a Cambridge education available to all, with no prior qualifications needed. So what would you like to learn today? "I was impressed by the accessible course format of the online course, especially during lockdown, ensuring that I could devote full attention to the enriching content. The course felt fully interactive; I enjoyed the opportunity to interact directly with leading experts in their field and fellow students. It has been very effective in shedding new perspectives on the topic and ensured that I was able to remain engaged with my own area of academic interest during a period when other academic institutions had partially shut down."
